Elke Sommer

information about drink

Ingredients

  • Zubrowka Vodka
  • Passionfruit Syrup
  • Juice of half a lemon
  • Simple Syrup
  • Mint Syrup
  • Lemon wedge
  • Crushed Ice
  • Thomas Henry Ginger Beer
  1. Mix vodka, passionfruit syrup, juice of half a lemon, simple syrup & mint syrup in a shaker
  2. Add the crushed ice to the glass and pour the content of the shaker
  3. Top up with ginger beer and stir the content
  4. Garnish with a lemon wedge and finish up with a straw
