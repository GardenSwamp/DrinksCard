Gin&Tonic

information about drink

Ingredients

  • Bulldog Gin
  • Juice of half a lemon
  • Thomas Henry Tonic Water
  • Rosmary Leaf
  • Lemon wedge
  • Crushed Ice
  1. Add the juice of half a lemon (leave the wedge in the bottom of the glass)
  2. Add the crushed ice to glass and pour the gin
  3. Top up with tonic water and stir the content
  4. Garnish with a lemon wedge and a rosemary leaf and finish up with a straw
