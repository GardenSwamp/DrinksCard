Moscow Mule

information about drink

Ingredients

  • Zubrowka Vodka
  • Juice of half a lemon
  • Simple Syrup
  • Crushed ice
  • Ginger beer
  • Lemon wedge
  1. Add the the juice of half a lemon (leave the wedge in the bottom of the glass)
  2. Add the crushed ice to glass and pour the vodka & simple syrup
  3. Top up with ginger beer and stir the content
  4. Garnish with a lemon wedge and finish up with a straw
