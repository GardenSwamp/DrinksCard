Tom Collins

information about drink

Ingredients

  • Bulldog Gin
  • Juice of half a lemon
  • Club Soda
  • Simple Syrup
  • Lemon wedge
  • Crushed Ice
  1. Add the juice of half a lemon (leave the wedge in the bottom of the glass)
  2. Add the crushed ice to glass and pour the gin and simple syrup
  3. Top up with club soda and stir the content
  4. Garnish with a lemon wedge and finish up with a straw
